# Privacy Policy

JLPT N4 Tutor is a static, on-device study app. We collect nothing.

## What stays on your device

- All study progress (which kanji you've marked known, which patterns you've reviewed, your test scores, your streak).
- All settings (theme, font, locale, audio rate).
- All weak-area data.

These live in your browser's `localStorage` under the namespace `jlpt-n4-tutor:*`. They never leave your device.

## What we send to servers

- Nothing. Zero analytics. Zero telemetry. No third-party scripts. No remote API calls during normal use.
- The only network traffic is the initial download of the app's static assets from GitHub Pages.

## Cross-device sync

We don't have a backend, so there is no cloud sync. Use the **Settings → Export progress** button to download your data as a JSON file; **Settings → Import progress** restores it on another device or browser.

## Cookies

None.

## Accounts

None. There is no login, no email collection, no account creation.

## Reset

**Settings → Reset all progress** wipes every `jlpt-n4-tutor:*` key from `localStorage`. Cannot be undone (export first if you want a backup).

## Questions

See the Feedback page in the app, or open an issue on the repo. The recipient address is obfuscated in source (decoded only at click-time) so it isn't harvested by scrapers.
